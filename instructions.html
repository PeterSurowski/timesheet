Instructions

This app lets you keep track of your employees' basic information, including their full names, their job titles, when they started working at your company, how much they make and how much your company has paid them to date.

To use it, fill in the fields in the "Add an employee" section and hit "submit." This will save all the info you entered in a database. To access the info, enter either a first, last or the full name of an employee in the search bar in the upper-right corner of your screen and hit "submit." The info to all the employees who match that name will appear on the screen.

The database where the info is stored can be edited here.
