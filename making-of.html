Making of "Timesheet"

This app is designed to keep track of an employee's basic information, including their full names, their start dates, how much they are paid, how long they have been working at the company and how much the company has paid the person to date.

Web develoer Peter Surowski created the app using the programming languages HTML, CSS and JavaScript. He also used the Bootstrap framework and the jQuery library. He used the Moments.js API to help with the date calculations and formating and he used Firebase as his database for storing the employee information entered by the user.

It took him about four hours and was built as part of his course work in The Coding Bootcamp at University of California, Irvine.
